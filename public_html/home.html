ICC Cricket World Cup 2015

Australia + New Zeland

The 2015 ICC Cricket World Cup will be the 11th ICC Cricket World Cup, and will be jointly hosted by Australia and New Zealand and is scheduled to take place from 14 February to 29 March 2015..

The Great War.....

The India Pakistan cricket rivalry is one of the most intense sports rivalries in the world. The 2011 World Cup Semifinal attracted around 1.5 billion TV viewers, the largest television event of the year.
